Fort Pilar is a 17th century military defense fortress built by the spanish colonial government in zamboanga city. outside the wall is an outdoor shrine dedicated to our Lady of Pilar. This is a major landmark of the city and symbol of its rich cultural heritage. Fort Pilar is rich in faith, history and spirit. Many people including tourists flock to this this spiritual attraction to renew their faith.
Tourists, balikbayans and locals devotees usually visit fort pilar to pray and witness the rich historical and cultural heritage of Fort Pilar.
This is one of the most popular place to visit in Zamboanga. People usually buy candles before going in, because you have to light up the candles as part of your prayers and thanksgiving.
