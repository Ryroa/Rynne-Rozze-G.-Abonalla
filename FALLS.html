Merloquet Falls is a stunning waterfall that flows down a rock formation and is surrounded by lush flora, the water there is very refreshing.Merloquet Falls is also a place where many people go to have picnics with their families. Merloquet Falls is located at Sibulao Brgy Road, Zamboanga, Zamboanga Sibugay, Philippines.
It is about 68 kilometers east of Zamboanga City in Sibulao. The falls are accessible by a lovely drive through the countryside and a short trek through the forest.
Merloquet Falls is a lovely site that provides a tranquil and pleasant respite from the rush. Its breathtaking natural beauty, swimming possibilities, and peaceful environment make it a must-see for anybody visiting Zamboanga.
