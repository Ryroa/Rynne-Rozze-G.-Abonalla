Lantawan Grassland, Zamboanga City. Here's where you can find the best view of Zamboanga City. Grassland is located not so far from the slope mountain of Zamboanga City. The road is rough in the entrance going up but the road after that is already in good shape. The view on the top is breathtaking you can see the whole city. The perfect time for you to go there is during sunset as you can see the beauty of the city from above during daylight.
